Fletcher Cove Renovations Complete

Written By: Rourke Blignaut Date Written: 2/22/26

The Fletcher Cove beach access renovations have also been completed. Originally starting in October, the renovations added showers at the bottom of the ramp, new railing, and a new storm drain dissipator grate. These renovations have been long needed due to the steepness of the ramp leading to the beach that acted as a risk to beachgoers. While not changing the steepness of the ramp, the renovations did change the concrete.
