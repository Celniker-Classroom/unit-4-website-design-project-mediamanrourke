Del Mar adds Sand Hole Restrictions

Written By: Rourke Blignaut. Date Written: 4/18/26

The City of Del Mar has added restrictions to digging holes on the beach. The new rules include: No digging holes deeper than 2ft, no burying people, all holes must be monitored at all times, and you must fill in the hole before you leave. This has sparked controversey among many beachgoers as digging holes is one of the most popular beach activites.
