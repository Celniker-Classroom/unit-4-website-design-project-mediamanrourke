D-Street Re-Opens After Years of Renovations

Written By: Rourke Blignaut Date Written: 2/22/26

the D Street access ramp in Ensenas has finally reopened after years of renovations. The access ramp is a much faster way to access Moonlight Beach, featuring showers and an overlook of the beach. Because D Street is a very popular access point, the renovations have been long awaited.
