Fletcher Cove Renovations Complete

Written By: Rourke Blignaut

The renovations to Fletcher Cove have been completed. The renovations added showers at the bottom of the access ramp and replaced the rusted railing and the concrete on the ramp. It also added a new storm disapater grate.
