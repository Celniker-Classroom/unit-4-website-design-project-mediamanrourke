DMVA Hosts Beach Clean Up

Written By: Rourke Blignaut Date Written: 4/18/26

The DMVA held a beach cleanup on 4/18/26. The cleanup was held on 1700 blvd in Del Mar and ran from 9:30-11am. Volunteers received panakes afterwards. The cleanup is held every 3 months.
